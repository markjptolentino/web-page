Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the art of defending systems, networks, and data against malicious threats. In a world of evolving cyberattacks, understanding security principles is crucial for protecting sensitive information and infrastructure.

  • Core Concepts: Encryption (AES, RSA), Firewalls, Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS), Virtual Private Networks (VPNs)
  • Threats: Malware, Phishing, Ransomware, DDoS Attacks
  • Tools: Wireshark (network analysis), Metasploit (exploitation), Nmap (port scanning), Snort (IDS)
  • Certifications: CISSP (expert-level), CEH (ethical hacking), CompTIA Security+ (entry-level), CCSP (cloud security)
  • Best Practices: Regular updates, strong passwords, multi-factor authentication (MFA), and zero-trust architecture
